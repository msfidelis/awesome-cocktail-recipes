> Beautiful Drink for Hot Days...

## Considerations

* Shaken Drink
* Put sparking water after shaking

## Ingredients

* 50 ml Gin
* 25 ml lemon juice (siciliano)
* 30 ml hibiscus syrup
* sparkling water

## Decoration

* Hibiscus flower
