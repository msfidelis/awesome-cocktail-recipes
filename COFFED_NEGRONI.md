> My favorite drink in the world

## Considerations

* Stirred Drink
* Big ice cube
* Orange Oleo
* Aeropress or Hario v60

## Ingredients

* 30 ml Gin
* 45 ml Vermute Rosso
* 30 ml Campari
* 3g Coffee 
* Orange Bitters

## Decoration

* Orange
